Dog Fancy Event 2024

The 125th annual Dog Fancy Event is being held in Brazil on December 21st. This is the fanciest dog event of the year, starring over 300 different dogs. This event was made to show brazil how great dogs really are.This event is a tribute to the exceptional bond between humans and their canine companions, celebrating their loyalty, intelligence, and companionship. There will be treats at the walk in, and for the dog owners there will be a buffet in the lounge. We thank you for coming to our event site, and hope to see you on December 21st. Thank you.


Featured Dogs

  • Marley
  • Lassie
  • Balto
  • Pudsey


These dogs will be the showrunners this year. Marley has a long history in dog events, and is looking forward to this year's dog Fancy Event. Lassie is a world-class runner, and will be showing his new techniques. Balto likes to lay around, and will practice his abilities. Pudsey was invited because her mom made a fit about not coming.
